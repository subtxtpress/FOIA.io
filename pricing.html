FAQ

Pricing questions

How is the free plan limited?
The free plan allows up to 5 active requests at a time. All core features—letter drafting, the agency directory, deadline tracking, and notes—are included.
What does Pro add?
Pro ($5/month) removes the request limit and adds professional appeal letters and priority email support.
Is there a trial?
You can use the Core plan free forever with no credit card required. Upgrade to Pro whenever you need it.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel Pro at any time from your account settings. You won't be charged for the next billing cycle.
